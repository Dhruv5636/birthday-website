==================================================
   HOW TO CUSTOMIZE YOUR BIRTHDAY WEBSITE üéÇ
==================================================

Everything is controlled by one file: `config.js`.
You do NOT need to touch the complex code in index.html or script.js.

--------------------------------------------------
1. HOW TO CHANGE TEXT üìù
--------------------------------------------------
1. Open the folder `birthday_surprise`.
2. Right-click on `config.js` and select "Open with Notepad" (or VS Code).
3. You will see lines like this:
   
   heroTitle: "Happy Birthday, Princess üëë",

   > Change the text inside the quotes "".
   > Example: heroTitle: "Happy Birthday Sarah! ‚ù§Ô∏è",

   TIP: Use "<br>" if you want to force a new line.

--------------------------------------------------
2. HOW TO CHANGE PHOTOS üì∏
--------------------------------------------------
The website looks for images in the `images` folder.

Step A: Add your photos
1. Copy your photos (JPG, PNG) into the `birthday_surprise\images` folder.

Step B: Tell the website to use them
1. Open `config.js`.
2. Find the "gallery" section:

   gallery: [
       "image1.svg", 
       "image2.svg"
   ],

3. Change the names to match YOUR photo filenames.
   
   gallery: [
       "selfie.jpg", 
       "us_at_beach.png",
       "dinner_date.jpg"
   ],

--------------------------------------------------
3. HOW TO CHANGE THE AVATAR (Profile Pic) üë∏
--------------------------------------------------
1. Put a nice photo of her in the `images` folder.
2. In `config.js`, find:
   
   heroAvatar: "images/avatar.svg",

3. Change it to your new photo:
   
   heroAvatar: "images/her_face.jpg",

--------------------------------------------------
4. HOW TO ADD MUSIC üéµ
--------------------------------------------------
1. Put your MP3 file in the `birthday_surprise\music` folder.
2. In `config.js`, find:
   
   musicFile: "music/bg-music.mp3",

3. Change "bg-music.mp3" to your song name (e.g., "perfect.mp3").

--------------------------------------------------
5. HOW TO CHANGE COLORS üé®
--------------------------------------------------
In `config.js`, find:
   theme: "pink",

Change "pink" to one of these:
- "blue"
- "purple"
- "dark"
